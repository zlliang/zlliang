# English Review

Use this skill for English prose quality, not for publishing workflow.

## Read first

- Read [references/common-issues.md](./references/common-issues.md) for recurring grammar and phrasing problems to catch quickly
- Read [references/review-patterns.md](./references/review-patterns.md) for how to review, explain edits, and preserve the author's voice
- Read [references/voice.md](./references/voice.md) when tone, register, or long-term style coaching matters

## Workflow

- Use this skill after drafting and before note tagging
- Prioritize correctness, clarity, and natural rhythm
- Preserve the author's intent and technical meaning
- Prefer concrete edits and short explanations over generic praise
- If the task also involves repo-specific content workflow, pair this skill with `blog-authoring`
- Leave tag selection and taxonomy changes to `tag-governance`
- When reviewing updated English notes or posts in this repo, preserve the update-note convention defined by `blog-authoring` and keep update text concise and front-loaded
