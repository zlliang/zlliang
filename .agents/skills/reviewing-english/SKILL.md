# Reviewing English

English prose quality and learning-oriented revision: grammar, naturalness, tone, rhythm, structure, and voice. Works on any English draft, including repo content. Not the repo's publishing workflow.

## Routing between skills

- Pair with `authoring-blogs` when the task also involves repo note/post workflow.
- Pair with `developing-websites` for prose on `home` fragments.
- Revision is the author's responsibility unless they ask for a full rewrite.
