I just added a new note type called "[collection notes](/notes/types/collection)".

Collection notes gather resources on a single topic — books, articles, videos — in one place. Think of them as better-organized bookmark folders. Here's the commit: [zlliang/zlliang@db12925](https://github.com/zlliang/zlliang/commit/db12925275ddcb446b700254a1a9cfb22d0c8584).
